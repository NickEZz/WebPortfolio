About Forester

Forester

Forester is an idle game, where you grow and chop trees to earn money to buy more land and build houses for workers. There is no set goal for the game, you can set your own goals to achieve in the game or just keep playing as long as you want. I created all 3D graphics for the game i also helped creating the UI buttons. I was also in charge of trailer.
